How It Works

  1. Choose whether to encipher (encode) or decipher (decode) a message.
  2. Pick a cipher algorithm from the list.
  3. Enter a key. The key is a secret between the sender and receiver!
  4. Enter your message, and the transformed output appears while you type.
  5. You can email your message or send it as a text using the bottom menus.
  6. Use the "reset" bottom menu to clear your message to keep it from prying eyes.

Tips

  • By tradition, the plaintext message is in lower case, while the ciphertext is in upper case.
  • You can learn more about the ciphers by tapping the "info" icon.
  • Punctuation and spaces for some ciphers are passed straight through. If you want to make your message a little harder to crack, leave them out.
  • You can copy your output message to the clipboard by just tapping on it.
